Who We Are?

Optimization of Rocket Engine and Structural Vibration (ORESV) as a pioneering research group dedicated to developing Rotating Detonation Engines (RDEs) for space and commercial transportation vehicles. The group's primary objectives include designing rocket engines and mitigating structural vibrations to foster innovation and advance the frontiers of space engineering.

About Us

At ORESV, we are driven by a passion for pioneering research in aerospace engineering. Our research interests extend to the exploration of detonation engines (DEs) for space applications. The DE engines represent a revolutionary approach to propulsion systems, offering numerous advantages over conventional methods. With our early research efforts, we aim to unlock the full potential of detonation engines and pave the way for their adoption in various aerospace applications.

Benefits of Detonation Engines: Detonation engines have the potential to redefine the future of propulsion technology. They offer superior performance, efficiency, and versatility compared to traditional airbreathing and rocket propulsion systems. The primary benefit of detonation engines is their higher thrust-to-weight ratios and greater specific impulse, which leads to higher efficiency and lower cost. The engines are also capable of providing greater thrust for longer durations.

Objectives

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance the development and implementation of detonation engines for space propulsion while addressing the challenges associated with high vibration levels. We are dedicated to ensuring the safety, reliability, and success of launch vehicles by mitigating the impact of broadband, severe, aero-acoustic, and structure-borne excitations. Through our research and innovation, we aim to safeguard the electronic survivability of payloads and vehicles, ultimately contributing to mission success and the advancement of space exploration.

Our Vision

ORESV is to push the boundaries of space exploration and engineering innovation. We are driven by a commitment to integrate detonation engines (DEs) into launch vehicles, revolutionizing space travel. By harnessing the power of DEs and other advanced technologies, we aim to redefine the capabilities of space propulsion systems, opening new frontiers of exploration and discovery.
